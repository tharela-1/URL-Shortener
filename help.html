How to use this website?
  • Step - 1: Enter the actual URL
  • Step - 2: Set how long you want the link to be active. Note that all the fields: Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds are required fields.
    For example:
    1. 6 days 4 hours 5 minutes 2 seconds can be given as - 6 in the days box, 4 in the hours box, 5 in the minutes box and 2 in the seconds box.
    2. 6 days 10 minutes 2 seconds can be given as - 6 in the days box, 0 in the hours box, 5 in the minutes box and 2 in the seconds box.
    and similarly for all other cases also.
    NOTE: If you enter a TTL less than 30 seconds i.e., less than 0 days 0 hours 0 minutes 30 seconds, then the TTL is set to 30 seconds to give a meaningful lifecycle for the generated URL.
  • Step - 3: Press the Generate Short URL button and get the shortened URL in the below box
